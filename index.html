Canagro Foods.

Agricultural Services and Products

Canagro Foods is a Canadian exporter and merchandiser.

We move our commodities directly from Farmers, Processors and manufacturers. We pack processed and unprocessed goods in PP bags (25kg, 50kg, Big Bags) or load pre-inspected food grade containers. Canagro Foods is experienced at exporting and importing of food products all over the world.

  • We have been involved in international food trade for almost 10 years
  • Our trading partners are located across Asia, Europe and both Americas.
  • We undertake all export formalities, prepare documentation and certificates required by exporting countries.

We have a long time existing network of logistic partners in Canada, US, Turkey, China, India, Ukraine, Russia and other countries. Our knowledge and experience will make the buying process simple and smooth. Give us a call or send enquiry for pricing and availability and we will do our best to serve you.
